I'm Kendra Egert

I currently live near Akron, OH but am orginally from out west. I've spent most of my life in Arizona and high desert of California. Ohio rain and snow are treats for me. My grandparents started me in my tech career and later in graphic design. I helped my grandfather figure out how to use the computer. Later, I took our old (like 75 year old) photos scanned and worked to bring them back to life. My love of books and tech enabled me create book covers and web sites for authors.

Last year I attended We can Code IT and learned much more about creating websites and web applications. I am confident I can use my improved skills in different industries. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
